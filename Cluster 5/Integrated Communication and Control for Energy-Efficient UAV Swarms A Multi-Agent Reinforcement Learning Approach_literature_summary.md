# Novelties
- To improve the quality of UAV swarm-assisted communications in complex geographic environments, we propose an integrated communication and control co-design mechanism.
  - Evidence Section: `Method/Discussion context`
- Given the stringent energy constraints inherent in UAV swarms, our proposed mechanism is designed to optimize energy eﬀiciency while maintaining an equilibrium between equitable communi- cation rates for mobile ground users (GUs) and UAV energy expenditure.
  - Evidence Section: `Method/Discussion context`
- We formulate the joint resource allocation and 3D trajectory control problem as a Markov decision process (MDP), and develop a multi-agent reinforcement learning (MARL) framework to enable real-time coordinated actions across the UAV swarm.
  - Evidence Section: `Method/Discussion context`
