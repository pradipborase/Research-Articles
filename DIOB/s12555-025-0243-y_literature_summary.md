# Novelties
- The inner-loop PID control in each UAV can improve its control behavior.
  - Evidence Section: `Method/Discussion context`
- To realize the task in the presence of disturbances, the suggested DO-FTFC-WRC includes i) equivalent control to tackle the newly designed following task and compen- sate the estimated disturbance, and ii) switching control with time-varying gain to deal with extra uncertainties for enhancing robust performance.
  - Evidence Section: `Method/Discussion context`
- Finally, the simulations and experiments as compared with the nonlinear finite-time following control or the inner-loop PID control with/without extra wind condition for a representative forward, 180 degrees clockwise turning, then backward motions validate the effectiveness and superiority of the proposed DO-FTFC-WRC.
  - Evidence Section: `Method/Discussion context`

# Practical Implementation Disadvantages
- Without the original nonlinear complex dynamics, its outer loop is modeled by a second-order system with unknown distur- bances, including sensor noises, system uncertainties, turbulence caused by ground effect, or extra wind gusts.
  - Evidence Section: `Method/Discussion context`
- On the contrary, the navigation in the GPS-denied region, e.g., warehouse, library, will be a challenge.
  - Evidence Section: `Method/Discussion context`
- Hence, a multi-UAV cooperative optimization model or the coverage path planning in wireless networks (e.g., WiFi network) can improve surveillance or secure effi- ciency when obstructions of different scales appear [9-11].
  - Evidence Section: `Method/Discussion context`
