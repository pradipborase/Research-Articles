# Novelties
- By introducing a prediction-correction mech- anism, a new compensation function observer (CFO) is constructed to enhance the estimation accuracy of the total disturbance.
  - Evidence Section: `Method/Discussion context`
- Based on an inductive decoupling-based Lyapunov method, this article rigorously establishes the closed-loop stability of the CADRC under a broad class of nonlin- ear state-dependent uncertainties.
  - Evidence Section: `Method/Discussion context`
- Finally, the proposed CADRC is implemented on a Quanser two-degree-of-freedom helicopter system sub- ject to additional digital uncertainties, and the experimental results demonstrate its clear superiority over the conven- tional ADRC.
