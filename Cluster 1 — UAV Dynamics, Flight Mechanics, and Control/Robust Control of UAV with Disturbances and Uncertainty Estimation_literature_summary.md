# Novelties
- This controller ensures the tracking of desired references in the presence of parameters variation and external disturbances, making use of high-order sliding mode (HOSM) estimators to estimate these perturbations that can be canceled by the control, thus improving the dynamic behavior of the controlled system.

- Its performance is evaluated making use of a Simcenter Amesim quadrotor based on physical models generated from experimental data in a co-simulation framework with Matlab–Simulink used to implement the designed controller with FPGA implementation.
